Policies & Revisions

Front Desk office hours are 8:30 am to 10:00 pm. Fiber-Optic $$$$ Internet Feeds this remote Resort, currently we provide it Free. Cell phone coverage is also available in our area.

Breakfast: 8:30 am to 10:30 am - Please reserve a room service time. Lunch: 12:00 pm to 4:00 pm - Served/Take out/Room Service. Dinner: Please reserve a Table or Room Service time by calling Extension 108 between 1:00 pm - 5:00 pm the day of your arrival.

All Accommodations have Hot Tubs: Resort Policy for your Health Protection is that Hot Tub Chemical Testing is done daily in your Hot Tub, regardless of signage you hang at your door.

Cleaning will occur between 9 am and mid-afternoon. The cleaning card MUST be displayed on your outside door handle or in the plastic holder for housekeeping.

Business Services are available - Inquire at Front Desk.

Please no disturbing music around the lake front or from your private deck.

Keep rooms quiet after 12 midnight.

No Pets Allowed.

All rooms are non-smoking and non-vaping. There are ashtrays provided for smokers located on the balcony or patio. There will be a $300 surcharge applied, if you are smoking or vaping or leaving Cannabis in your accommodation. Leave Cannabis in your vehicle as it is known to stink up the accommodation. During the Spring, Summer and Fall there are Firepits set up by the lake for those that indulge, to enjoy.

All Damages to Accommodations and facilities are the guest's responsibility!

Please give privacy & respect to homes & cottages on the lake and on the Resort. Do Not Trespass.

Policies & Revisions

Power Outage Policy was introduced due to outage in winter 2009, the first time in 42 years that the resort had to shut down due to a power outage. Currently in a Power Outage we can Provide the Following Services:

1. Wood burning fireplace in all Accommodations; for Romance ( Possibly # 1 Reason Guests stay with us) 2. Fully operational Kitchen; Gourmet Dining (Possible also the #1 Reason Guests stay with us) 3. Cold running water in all Accommodations 4. Hot and Cold Showers in the Gym/Sauna Facilities 5. Recreation Room with Big TV for the Vital Game (Much lower on the list of reasons to stay with us...but May be critical) 6. Office has access to the outside world with Phone and Data Line services 7. We Apologize that all the Toys in your Accommodation, and the Pool/Hot Tub will Not be operational.

- We will try to update the home page of our website to tell when we are up again. - If there is a power outage we will try to notify guests. If we are successful the deposit will be refunded if desired. - If guests arrive or if a power outage happens while guests are here, we will offer: o If power goes out for two hours between 4pm and 11pm: 10% off o If power goes out for three hours between 8pm and 11pm: 20% off o If power goes out for four hours between 7pm and 11pm: 30% off o If power goes out for five hours between 6pm and 11pm: 35% off and one free night stay in the future, similar accommodation o Power outages after 11pm through to 4pm will have no discount attached.
